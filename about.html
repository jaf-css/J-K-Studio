Our Story

Founded by Junniel Sorriano and Kathleen Duya, J&K Studio started with a passion for photography and a love for celebrations. What began as a small side hustle has grown into a trusted photo booth service, serving around Pampanga. We pride ourselves on delivering top-notch service with a personal touch—because your special moments deserve nothing less!
